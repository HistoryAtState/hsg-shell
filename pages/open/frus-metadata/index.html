Bibliographic Metadata of the Foreign Relations of the United States Series

The Office of the Historian is responsible, under law, for the preparation and publication of the official historical documentary record of U.S. foreign policy in the Foreign Relations of the United States series. This dataset is the raw bibliographic metadata for the nearly 500 volumes in the series published since 1861.
